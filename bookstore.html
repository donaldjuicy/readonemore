About the Shop

A little corner of the world for book lovers

⸻

The Dog-Eared Page opened its doors in 2009 in a narrow shopfront on Elm Street. What started as a few shelves of paperbacks and a pot of strong tea has grown into a beloved neighbourhood institution — a place where books change hands, conversations linger, and cats occasionally sit on the bestsellers.

We believe secondhand books carry something more than words: the ghost of a reader who came before. A margin note, a pressed flower, a receipt from 1987 — these are the things that make a book truly yours.

Every title on our shelves has been personally read, sorted, and priced by our small team. We update our stock weekly, and we're always buying — bring us your unwanted books and we'll give them a second life.
